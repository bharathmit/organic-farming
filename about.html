Our Story

Growing with Purpose & Passion

From a small family farm to a thriving organic community — here's the story of GreenRoots and why we do what we do.

🌳

A Farm Built on Principles

GreenRoots was born from a simple conviction: that the food we eat should be grown with respect — for the soil, for the farmers, and for the families who depend on it.

Founded in 2010 by the Nair family in the lush hills of Coorg, Karnataka, we started with just 20 acres and a dream to prove that organic farming could be both viable and scalable. Today, we farm over 500 acres across three certified organic estates.

We follow regenerative agriculture principles — building soil health, conserving water, supporting biodiversity, and growing food that is as nutritious as it is delicious.

Core Values

What We Stand For

Our values aren't just words — they guide every seed we plant and every decision we make.

🌍

Environmental Stewardship

We treat the land as a living system. Our regenerative practices improve soil health, sequester carbon, and protect local ecosystems for future generations.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Community First

We employ over 300 local farming families, pay fair wages above market rates, and invest in education, healthcare, and housing in rural communities.

🔬

Science-Led Organic

We combine ancient farming wisdom with modern agroecology research to continuously improve crop quality, yield, and resilience to climate change.

✅

Full Transparency

Every product carries a traceable farm code. Scan it to see exactly which plot your food came from, who grew it, and when it was harvested.

💚

Zero Waste Goal

We compost all farm waste, run biogas units powered by crop residue, and are on track to achieve zero-landfill status by 2027.

🌤️

Climate Resilience

Through diversified cropping systems and water conservation, we've reduced our vulnerability to extreme weather by 60% over the last 5 years.

Our Journey

Milestones Along the Way

Fifteen years of growing, learning, and making a difference.

2010

GreenRoots is Founded

The Nair family converts a conventional 20-acre farm to organic, obtaining their first certification from APEDA (Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority).

2013

First Direct-to-Consumer Delivery

We launch weekly veggie box deliveries to 50 families in Bengaluru — the seed of what would become our farm-to-door service.

2016

100-Acre Milestone & Community Farm

We expand to 100 certified organic acres and launch a community-supported agriculture (CSA) program, empowering 5 neighboring families to adopt organic practices.

2019

Online Platform Launch

GreenRoots.in goes live, allowing customers across South India to order fresh organic produce with same-day harvesting and next-day delivery.

2022

500 Acres & National Expansion

We reach 500 organic acres across three estates and begin serving customers in Mumbai, Pune, and Hyderabad. National award for Best Organic Farm Initiative.

2025

12,000+ Customers & Carbon Neutral

We achieve carbon-neutral certification and cross 12,000 monthly active customers. Launch of the GreenRoots Farm School for agri-education.
