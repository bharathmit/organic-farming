Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

🌿 Are your products really 100% organic?

Yes. All our produce is certified by APEDA and NPOP (India Organic). We undergo annual third-party audits and every product has a traceable farm code you can verify online.

🚚 How long does delivery take?

Order before 10 AM and we harvest same day. Delivery is next day for Bengaluru, Chennai, Mumbai, Hyderabad, and Pune. Other cities within 2 days.

🔄 What if I'm not satisfied with freshness?

We stand behind our freshness guarantee 100%. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 24 hours of delivery and we'll replace or fully refund your order — no questions asked.

📦 Do you do bulk / B2B orders?

Absolutely! We supply organic produce to restaurants, hotels, hospitals, and corporate canteens at special rates. Email partner@greenroots.in or call us to discuss your requirements.

🏡 Can I visit the farm?

Yes! We run guided farm tours on Saturdays. It's a wonderful experience for families, students, and nature lovers. Book via our contact form or call us at least one week in advance.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), all major credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery for orders under ₹2,000 in select cities.
